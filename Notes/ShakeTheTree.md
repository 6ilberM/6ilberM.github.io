# Shake the tree Task Log

## Tasks

- [ ] Tree growth rate: the improvement shows the growth of a tree from a small seedling to the size you have now. The money of this block is earned in tapas on small buds (on tap they bring income, open and turn into a twig and leaves; when you upgrade the next block of money is collected already from the leaves, for example, etc.).
- [ ]fertility : the rate at which buds, leaves, fruits appear.
- [ ]heap :increase in buds, leaves, fruits.
- [ ] Immediately enter the concept of fertiliser - a separate button, with a constant tap on which doubles the speed of all indicators related to growth and income of money. This button has a function, for viewing ads can fix the effect of doubling for 3 minutes.
- [ ] Immediately enter the concept of increasing profits - a separate button, which for viewing ads gives an increase in profits from any action for 2 minutes
- [ ] Visually, you'll need to make the opacity of the tree's crown where the fruit appears.
There's no need to shake the tree. But you can leave a slight tremor at the tapé on the apple.
